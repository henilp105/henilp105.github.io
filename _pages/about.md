I am **Henil Panchal**, an AI engineer and financial analytics researcher currently pursuing an MSc in Financial Analytics at Queen's University Belfast (GIFT City Campus).

My work spans multi-agent AI systems, LLM robustness/security, quantitative finance, and production ML platforms on GCP. I have contributed across research and industry roles including Queen's University Belfast, BITS Pilani, TCS Research, and UIUC (Daniel Kang Lab).

I am a **NAACL'25 TrustNLP Spotlight** co-author for work on indirect prompt injection attacks in LLM agents, and an active open-source contributor through Fortran-lang (GSoC'22, backend and developer contributions to fpm).
